# Daisy Printing — Business Manager
### What's New · 15 April 2026

---

## ✅ Bugs Fixed

**Tabs not working after saving**
After saving and reopening the file, only the Dashboard was accessible — clicking Inventory, Pricing, Invoices or Business Insights did nothing. Fixed.

**Dashboard showing wrong figures**
The dashboard was displaying the original starter data instead of real saved data. Fixed — all figures now correctly reflect your actual invoices, products and inventory.

**Table headers missing**
The column headings on the Invoices, Inventory and Pricing tables were invisible. Fixed — headers now appear as a clear pink bar at the top of every table.

**Paid invoices showing as "Pending"**
Invoices marked as paid were incorrectly showing a Pending badge. Fixed — Paid shows in green, Pending shows in amber, and the action buttons clearly say "Mark Paid" or "Mark Unpaid".

**Business Insights not updating with new invoices**
New invoices weren't being picked up by the Business Insights charts and figures. Fixed — everything updates the moment a change is made anywhere in the app.

## ✨ New Features

**Supplier column in Inventory**
Each inventory item now has a Supplier field you can fill in directly in the table — no need to open the edit screen. Just click and type.

**Order paid checkbox in Inventory**
A checkbox next to each inventory item lets you mark whether the stock order has been paid for.

**Supplier Insights section**
At the bottom of the Inventory tab there is now a Supplier Insights panel showing:
- Total spend per supplier as a bar chart
- A table of outstanding (unpaid) orders with amounts owed
- A full per-supplier breakdown showing paid vs unpaid status

**Quotes**
You can now create quotes alongside invoices. Key details:
- Quotes are numbered separately — Q001, Q002, and so on
- The printed document is clearly marked "QUOTE — Not a Tax Invoice" with a "Valid for 30 days" note
- One click converts any quote into an invoice, which gets the next available invoice number automatically
- Quotes are excluded from your revenue figures — only converted invoices count in your Dashboard and Business Insights

**Lead source tracking**
Invoices and quotes can now be tagged with where the customer came from — Online, Direct, Referral, Social Media, or Repeat Client. You can set it when creating a new record, or update any existing invoice or quote from the list without opening it.

**Leads & Quote Pipeline in Business Insights**
A new section at the bottom of Business Insights shows:
- How many quotes were created and how many became invoices
- Your overall conversion rate
- Revenue broken down by lead source
- A list of open quotes still waiting, colour-coded by age (green = recent, amber = getting old, red = overdue)

When you click Save & Download, the file is named with the current date and time — e.g. `Business Tracker 15 April 07:07.html` — so it's always clear which version is the latest.
